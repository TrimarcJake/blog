Jake Hildreth is a dedicated husband, fun-loving father, and seasoned IT professional with nearly 25 years of experience. As a Senior Security Consultant at Trimarc, Jake leads the Active Directory (AD) Security Assessment service and helps organizations fortify their digital defenses.

His open-source tools—Locksmith, BlueTuxedo, and PowerPUG!—are designed to lighten the load for overworked AD administrators by making security more accessible and manageable. Jake's expertise is further underscored by his CISSP certification, a testament to his wide base of knowledge and commitment to cybersecurity excellence.
